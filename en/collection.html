Local Collection

Local Collection is a unique selection of objects created in Tortel that translate the Patagonian landscape into one-of-a-kind pieces. We work with materials from the territory—local woods, lichens, and pressed flowers and ferns—transforming them into contemporary crafts that preserve the essence of Chilean Patagonia. We do not mass-produce. Each piece is unrepeatable and reflects the bond between nature, craftsmanship, and local memory.

Botanical Resin

Resin accessories with flowers and lichens from Patagonia

A line of accessories crafted from epoxy resin that encapsulates botanical elements responsibly gathered from the forests and gardens of Tortel.

Lichens, ferns, and pressed flowers remain suspended in transparency, preserving the unique shapes and hues of the Patagonian ecosystem.

The result is a fragment of the landscape transformed into an object.

Native Wood

Wooden boards and objects crafted in Tortel

Wood is a structural part of Tortel's history. This collection brings together serving boards and utilitarian objects crafted using traditional techniques and local woods, respecting their organic grain, textures, and shapes.

Each piece expresses the woodworking craft that shaped the territory's identity, connecting functionality with natural aesthetics. All pieces in the Local Collection are unique and part of a living catalog that changes with the seasons and creative processes.

If you are looking for authentic crafts from Tortel or an object with Patagonian identity, contact us to discover our current collection.
