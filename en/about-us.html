About Us

Travesía Local is a creative tourism agency based in Caleta Tortel, offering authentic experiences inspired by local life, nature, and heritage.

Through tailored tours, workshops, and cultural routes, we invite you to discover Patagonia from its very essence, connecting with the people, craftsmanship, and landscapes that make this territory unique.

At Travesía Local, we are a team of hosts, sailors, and storytellers committed to offering unique travel experiences in Tortel, designed from a deep knowledge of the land and a profound respect for its identity.

We do not just guide tours; we create immersive experiences in Tortel that allow you to understand the territory from within. Each journey is carefully designed to offer authenticity, safety, and a narrative that connects visitors with the essence of Chilean Patagonia.

We focus on:

  • Exclusive and personalized experiences
  • A professional and committed team
  • Stories and insights you won’t find on mass tourism circuits
  • A genuine connection with Tortel’s identity and culture

Travesía Local is your gateway to a living, navigable, and deeply human Patagonia.
